Peter Griffin

Fun Facts about Peter Griffin

  • His full name is Peter Löwenbräu Griffin.
  • He is of Irish, Mexican descent.
  • He is married to Lois, the daughter of the wealthy Pewterschmidt family.
  • He has three children; Meg, Chris, and Stewie.
  • He is the biological father of Bertram, Stewie’s frenemy.
  • Peter had a son previously with Lois. Peter Griffin Jr died after Peter shook him too much.
  • His “best buddy” is an adopted dog, Brian.
  • His best friends and neighbors are; sex manic Glen Quagmire, paraplegic police officer Joe Swanson and African-American Cleveland Brown.
  • The Griffins live on Spooner Street in Quahog, Rhode Island.
  • He was once best friends with Hollywood actor James Woods.
  • His paternal father is an Irishman, Mickey McFinnigan, the town drunk.
  • Francis Griffin (Peter’s stepfather) is a devout Catholic and often comments on Peter’s lifestyle.
  • Peter is clinically obese, weighing in at 270 lbs.
  • Proud of his weight, Peter created the coalition for fat men in Fat Guy Strangler; he is nearly killed by Lois’ long-lost brother.
  • His intelligence is so low he is classed as mentally retarded.
  • Peter’s had many jobs, including; towel boy, toy maker, fisherman, office lackey, and starting center of the New England Patriots football team.
  • He wrote a number of erotic novels.
  • Peter is a fan of the band KISS and is incredibly proud when Lois reveals she once slept with Gene Simmons.
  • Peter broadcast his own television station after the FCC ban; he called it PTV.
  • His favorite song is the Trashmen’s “Surfin’ Bird.”
  • He often fights Ernie, the Giant Chicken.
  • He has out-farted filmmaker, author, and political commentator, Michael Moore.
